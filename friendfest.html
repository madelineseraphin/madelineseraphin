FriendFest Site

2016, for Interaction Design 1 class

I created and designed a social media platform for music lovers to buy and sell concert tickets and discover more live music events in their area. I went through the process of making wireframes, user testing those wireframes, then creating high-fidelity sketches of the interface in mobile, tablet, and desktop widths. You can visit a working prototype of the mobile site or the desktop site.
